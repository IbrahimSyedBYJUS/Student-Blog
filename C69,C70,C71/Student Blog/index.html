Places that you must visit

LABRANDA MARES RESORT, Marmaris, Türkiye

I stayed in this resort from October 21st 2022 to October 28th 2022

This is the best resort in Marmaris in Türkiye. It is a full resort, not just a hotel. In my time there, my family and I visited the dolphin show that they had, which was very fun. Then we decided to swim in their swimming pool. Every morning, we ate the best breakfast we could ever have in Türkiye, and for lunch and dinner it was the same. You must go to this resort, it's cheap and affordable, but it is a great experience, with kind staff and good service.

COX'S BAZAR - SEA PEARL BEACH RESORT

I stayed in this resort from April 17th 2018 to April 21st 2018

Cox's Bazar Beach is located at Cox's Bazar, Bangladesh, is the longest natural sea beach in the world running 120 kilometres (75 mi)and 5th longest beach after Praia do Cassino of Brazil, Padre Island on the US Gulf Coast, Eighty Mile Beach in Western Australia, and Ninety Mile Beach of Australia. It is the top tourist destination of Bangladesh.

Review on movies

Sonic The Hedgehog 2
Great film, must watch.

This movie is a great one to watch. Though, if you are to watch it, you require some background, as the movie entirely revolves around the first movie - Sonic the Hedgehog 1. After you watch that, the storyline and the character backgrounds become clear, and the movie becomes a treat to watch.

{ SPOILERS - Read at your own risk } The movie begins with Sonic enjoying time, and his ' parents ' leave. Then he meets Tails and they venture Siberia, then they go to a beach and get the ' Master Emerald '. The villain of the movie takes the emerald and then Sonic gets the emerald back, and defeats the villain. Then Sonic goes back home and celebrates.

Harry Potter and the Order of the Pheonix
Best Harry Potter Movie - Must watch.

This is the best Harry Potter movie. You need a little bit of background on the previous movies, but not all. This movie is a perfect example of a good book to film adaptation. The story is better than the book. The book is the longest one in the series, and the movie emcompasses all of that information into a 2 hour film. The movie takes out the unneccessary parts of the book, and improves the characters, giving them more important roles, but it doesn't stray that far away from the movie canon.
